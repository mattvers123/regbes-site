Technical resources

Shore Power Tools for Port Engineers and Vessel Operators

Free technical tools to size, configure, and validate your shore power system — from IEC 80005 cable selection and reel sizing to real-time port and vessel compatibility assessment. Built by the REGBES engineering team.

Available tools
Two tools. Every stage of your project.

Use the sizing tool to configure your barge-to-ship cable management system. Use the compatibility checker to validate your port's equipment against your target vessel. Both free, no login required for the public modules.

Live — free to use /resources/bluebarge-sizing-tool
BlueBARGE System Sizing Calculator

Size your barge-to-ship shore power system — select your target vessel type, get the applicable IEC 80005 standard, validate cable count, and configure your reel setup. Cost estimates are delivered by the REGBES team on request.

Shore Power Availability & Compatibility Checker

A multi-module platform that checks whether a port's shore power equipment is compatible with a target vessel — covering IEC standards, voltage class, plug type, power capacity, and weather operability. Enter your port's equipment, select your target vessel, and get an instant compatibility assessment.
